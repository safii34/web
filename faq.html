Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Forex Account Management services.

What is Trading Account Management?

Trading Account Management is a service where experienced traders manage investment accounts using professional trading strategies and risk management systems.

How does Forex Account Management work?

Clients provide access to a trading account while professional managers execute trades according to agreed risk parameters and investment objectives.

What is Risk Management?

Risk management focuses on protecting capital by controlling position size, leverage and overall account exposure.

What is a PAMM Account?

PAMM (Percentage Allocation Management Module) allows investors to allocate funds to a professional trader while profits and losses are distributed proportionally.

What is a MAM Account?

MAM (Multi Account Manager) allows professional traders to manage multiple investor accounts simultaneously from one master account.

Do you guarantee profits?

No. No legitimate investment service can guarantee profits. Our focus is on disciplined risk management and long-term consistency.

Simply contact us through our contact page and we will discuss available account management solutions.
